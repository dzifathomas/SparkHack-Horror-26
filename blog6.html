Day 6: The Offer

Posted: Saturday, October 28 • 11:59 PM

She came clean. Her son died in this apartment decades ago. She feeds it tenants to keep its spirit "alive." Now she offers me a choice:

Become the new landlady (pass the curse along)

OR

Join the collection (become permanent decor)

What's your answer?

Your choice has been recorded.

Your choice will be honored. The walls are listening. The pipes remember. The wires never forget.
